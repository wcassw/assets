# Are you ready, arrival is sooner than expected !!

## [Romans 10:8-10‬](https://bible.com/bible/1/rom.10.8.KJV)
Salvation comes through openly confessing Jesus as Lord and truly believing that God raised Him from the dead. Faith is not only private belief; it is also a declared trust in Christ.

> “But what saith it? The word is nigh thee, even in thy mouth, and in thy heart: that is, the word of faith, which we preach;”‭‭ “that if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.”‭‭ “For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.”

## [Philippians 4:6–8](https://bible.com/bible/1/php.4.6.KJV)
Do not be ruled by worry. Bring every concern to God in prayer with thanksgiving, and His peace will guard your heart and mind through Christ Jesus.

> “Be careful for nothing; but in every thing by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known unto God.”
‭‭ “And the peace of God, which passeth all understanding, shall keep your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus.” “Finally, brethren, whatsoever things are true, whatsoever things are honest, whatsoever things are just, whatsoever things are pure, whatsoever things are lovely, whatsoever things are of good report; if there be any virtue, and if there be any praise, think on these things.”

## [Jeremiah 29:12–14](https://bible.com/bible/1/jer.29.12.KJV)
God promises to listen when His people pray and seek Him sincerely. When they search for Him with all their heart, He will be found and will restore them.

>  “Then shall ye call upon me, and ye shall go and pray unto me, and I will hearken unto you.”,  “And ye shall seek me, and find me, when ye shall search for me with all your heart.”   “And I will be found of you, saith the LORD: and I will turn away your captivity, and I will gather you from all the nations, and from all the places whither I have driven you, saith the LORD; and I will bring you again into the place whence I caused you to be carried away captive.”

## [1 Thessalonians 5:17–18](https://bible.com/bible/1/1th.5.17.KJV)
Believers are called to live in constant prayer and give thanks in every situation. Gratitude and prayer are part of God’s will for those in Christ.

> “Pray without ceasing.”  “In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you.”

## [1 John 1:8](https://bible.com/bible/1/1jn.1.8.KJV)
Anyone who claims to have no sin is deceiving themselves. Honest faith begins with admitting our need for God’s forgiveness.

> “If we say that we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us.” “If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.”

## [Galatians 2:20](https://bible.com/bible/1/gal.2.20.KJV)
The believer’s old life is crucified with Christ. A new life is lived by faith in Jesus, who loved us and gave Himself for us.

> “I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me: and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me.”

## [Joshua 1:8](https://bible.com/bible/1/jos.1.8.KJV)
God commands His Word to stay in our mouth, mind, and actions. Meditating on Scripture and obeying it leads to spiritual strength and success.

> “This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate therein day and night, that thou mayest observe to do according to all that is written therein: for then thou shalt make thy way prosperous, and then thou shalt have good success.”

## [John 14:21](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/JHN.14.21.KJV)
True love for Jesus is shown through obedience to His commandments. Those who love Him are loved by the Father, and Christ reveals Himself to them.

> He that hath my commandments, and keepeth them, he it is that loveth me: and he that loveth me shall be loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will manifest myself to him.

## [Psalm 100:2–4](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/PSA.100.2.KJV)

## [Matthew 25:40](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/MAT.25.40.KJV)
Jesus teaches that acts of kindness toward the needy are treated as service done to Him. Caring for others is a direct expression of faith.

> And the King shall answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye have done it unto one of the least of these my brethren, ye have done it unto me.

## [Proverbs 3:5–6](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/PRO.3.5.KJV)
Trust the Lord completely instead of relying only on your own understanding. Acknowledge Him in all areas of life, and He will guide your path.

> Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; And lean not unto thine own understanding.  In all thy ways acknowledge him, And he shall direct thy paths.

# KJV Bible Verses: A Nation Born in One Day

## Key Verse

[**Isaiah 66:8**](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/ISA.66.8.KJV)

> Who hath heard such a thing? who hath seen such things? Shall the earth be made to bring forth in one day? or shall a nation be born at once? for as soon as Zion travailed, she brought forth her children.

## Related Context

[**Isaiah 66:7**](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/ISA.66.7.KJV)

> Before she travailed, she brought forth; before her pain came, she was delivered of a man child.

[**Isaiah 66:9**](https://www.bible.com/bible/1/ISA.66.9.KJV)

> Shall I bring to the birth, and not cause to bring forth? saith the LORD: shall I cause to bring forth, and shut the womb? saith thy God.

## Summary

The main verse people refer to about Israel, or Zion, becoming a nation in one day is **Isaiah 66:8**. The passage uses the picture of sudden childbirth to describe Zion bringing forth her children quickly and unexpectedly.

The key phrase is:

> shall a nation be born at once?
